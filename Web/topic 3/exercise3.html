Brixton is known for its cultural diversity with a high proportion of residents living their being afro-carribean.

Brixton has electric avenue which was the first street in London to be electrified.

Brixton has its own currency called the Brixton pound.

Brixtons tate libary was one of the first free libaries in London.

Brixton is where David Bowie was born.

Brixton is named one of the birthplaces of reggae music.
